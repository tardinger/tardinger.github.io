Theo Ardinger is a designer and student based in San Francisco.

Hello, my name is Theo Ardinger. I’m a Design major at the University of San Francisco passionate about blending creativity and technology. While my education has given me a strong foundation in visual design, I’m particularly drawn to motion graphics and web design, where I can bring ideas to life in dynamic, interactive ways. I am currently working as a design intern at USF’s in-house creative team at the Office of Marketing Communications!

I’m driven by the desire to create work that is not only visually effective but also meaningful and fulfilling. Whether it’s designing a responsive website, animating engaging content, or experimenting with new creative tools, I’m excited by projects that challenge me to think outside the box.

Outside of work, I find inspiration in the world around me—whether I’m exploring nature, completing puzzles, visiting museums, or watching a great movie. These experiences fuel my creativity and help me approach design from fresh perspectives.

Let's connect if you’re interested in collaborating on creative projects or sharing ideas for anything Design!
